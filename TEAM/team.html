IOTA is the official student-run IoT and Embedded Systems club of Jadavpur University.

We are a community of passionate students exploring the world of Internet of Things, robotics, hardware prototyping, and innovation.

From organizing workshops and hackathons to building real-world tech projects, we aim to bridge theory with hands-on experience.

If you're excited about tech, teamwork, and turning ideas into impact — you're in the right place!
